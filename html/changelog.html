08 December 2022

Sennalen updated:

  • Fixed a runtime that could cause an exoplanet flora to lack reagents.

06 December 2022

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Zombie juice is no longer a spawnable option from data capsules.

05 December 2022

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Area boundaries for deck 1 and deck 3 aft hallways have been pushed back to line up with the physical boundaries created by the doors and windows in the hallways.
  • Disassembling racks no longer gives you free aluminum.
  • Guest pass terminals now allow guest passes to have a time limit of 60 minutes, up from 30 minutes.
  • You can now link PDAs to fax machines to receive notifications of received faxes from anywhere on the ship. You can do this by clicking the fax machine with your PDA.
  • You can no longer create new inflatables by spam clicking them.
  • Bunny slippers can now be found in the footwear section of character loadout.
  • Titanium (And other materials with high crafting costs) can now have their crafting menus opened again.
  • Claymores now use the correct material when they are crafted.
  • Mininghome now includes ground ores for the big drill.
  • Medbay has nanoblood again.
  • Prebuilt mechs, including the cargo powerloader, now have valid cameras in their sensors.

04 December 2022

SierraKomodo updated:

  • The locations of radio beacons have been tweaked to be less intrusive and resolve various issues of blocking access to other areas/items, or outright replacing them.
  • The bridge uniform vendor has been restored.
  • The SEV Aquila now has a radio beacon.
  • The Casino's radio beacon has been moved to the bridge area.
  • Radio beacons have been removed from the blueriver and oldpod maps.
  • Salvage Gantry's radio beacon has been moved to the rear section of the cockpit area.
  • Skrell ship's radio beacon has been moved to the bridge area.
  • Radio beacon has been removed from the Guppy pending a planned update to use wall-mounted beacons and a decision on whether or not the pod should even have one.
  • Added radio beacons to the mercenary ship, as well as the fleet and terran event ships.
